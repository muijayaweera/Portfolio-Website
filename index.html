OstoCare

OstoCare is a mobile application designed to improve the lives of ostomy patients by providing them with ongoing care, emotional support, and easy access to vital information. The app helps patients track their health, locate nearby hospitals and pharmacies, connect with stoma care nurses, and even record their progress. It was developed as part of a university project, aiming to tackle a real-world healthcare challenge in Sri Lanka.

The app is built using Flutter for cross-platform compatibility, with Firebase serving as the backend for real-time data storage and authentication. Its development followed the Agile methodology, ensuring iterative progress and adaptability throughout the project.

What makes OstoCare special is its dual focus: while it empowers patients with tools for daily care and emotional well-being, it also supports healthcare providers with access to patient data and teleconsultation features. It’s more than just an app—it’s a step towards bridging healthcare gaps for a vulnerable community.
